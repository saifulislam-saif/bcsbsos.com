F A Q

A. What is online registration process?

1. Click on the 'Register' button

2. Provide your name, mobile number, email address and a password of at least 6 digit.

3. Finaly Click 'Submit' button.

Note : Your email address will be your user ID.

B. How i watch videos?

Videos may be RECORDED or LIVE.

Live videos will be given to your profile and you can watch it later if you missed the LIVE session due to any unavoidable situation. (Plz click on "Online Lecture Links")

All videos are organised in your profile and videos.

1. Are password protected & the password will be shown just before your video start on the video frame. Passwords will remain there and you don't need to memorise them for recall.

2. Will remain for the whole time of your course period.

3. Can be watched whenever you want.

4. Can be watched so many times as your wish.

5. Can be paused at any moment & then be resumed again and again. When you start playing videos then software system registered with your IP address. Your SIF profile may be deactivated without any prior notice if you share with anyone.So be careful about your ID & Password. As all the videos will remain organised in your profile for whole duration so you don't need to download and we can't help regarding this.

C. Steps required to take a lecture class.

1. First open Google Chrome in the browser (Avoid UC Browser).

2. Enter the web site www.bcsbsos.com

3. Click Login and login with your Email ID and Password.

4. Click on Online Lecture Links according to the scheduled date and time given in My schedule in the dashboard of your profile.

5. Click on the name of the scheduled lecture.

6. As soon as you turn on the video, you will see the VIDEO Password on the screen and type it in the specified place and click Watch Video.

7. Videos may take up to 5 minutes to load from the server.

8. Enable high net speed to avoid video streaming and reduce video resolution from settings.

9. You can increase or decrease the playback speed at your convenience from the video settings option. (.75x, 1.25x etc)

10. By clicking on the Lecture Sheet of the Dashboard, you will be able to download the lecture sheet of your scheduled class.

11. There is an option to pause the video, you can pause the video if you need a short break for any reason. You can drag back and forth as per your convenience and need.

12. Even if you can't watch the lecture video during the routine time, you can watch it even after the scheduled time, but try to complete the watch before the next exam.

13. Use the COMPLAIN BOX on the dashboard for any IT related issues.

14. Sit with the necessary preparation before you sit in the online class. Fully charge the device (computer, laptop, tab or smartphone), make sure you have an internet connection, and refrain from exchanging calls on the device during class. You can keep a notebook with a pen, headphones or earphones.

D. How to appear Online Exam?

You can get the exams by clicking on Online Exams & Results Links in your profile Dashboard at www.bcsbsos.com

At a Glance Online Exam Rules:

1. Login to www.bcsbsos.com with user ID and password.

2. Click on the online exam & Results option from the dashboard of your profile. After selecting your course and proper discipline, click on Proceed option and the exam link will show.

3. After clicking on the Start now box, your test will start.

4. If the question is MCQ type, each option has T and F circles opposite it. Click T circle if you think True is reasonable or F circle if you think False is reasonable, you can click F circle if you want to change after clicking T circle. (Vice versa).

5. If the question is SBA Type, click on any circle. There are opportunities to change circles.

6. If you want to get the next question, click on the Next box. If you want to get the question again without answering any question at the moment, click on the TRY LATER box. After answering the last question of the serial, you will get the opportunity to answer the questions. For example: In the test of 50 questions, if you have TRY LATER the question No. 7, 10, 15, 25, 32, then after answering the last question No. 50, you will get the opportunity to answer the questions No. 7,10,15,25,32 again.

7. When the test is over, click on the Submit box.

8. Keep track of the time, if you drop the internet connection after the scheduled time or during the test, the test will auto-submit.

E. How can i view full question & result?

1. After completing your exam, by clicking on the Submit Button, your full question paper with Your Answer, Correct Answer, Discussion, Reference will be displayed in the Exam View.

2. Click on the View Result Option and now you can see your Result.

About Us

Bangladesh School of Oncoplastic Surgery (BSOS) :

Bangladesh School of Oncoplastic Surgery (BSOS) is the only specialized institute in Bangladesh.

BSOS offers following courses : Refresher's course

Bangladesh School of Oncoplastic Surgery (BSOS) is a national umbrella organisation providing cancer research & cancer information for the people of Bangladesh.

Objectives :

To provide quality cancer treatment

To make a centre of excellence in the country

To carry out research and disseminate the knowledge and findings

To establish a nationwide cancer registry system

To take necessary steps for cancer prevention and control

Activities :

All diagnostic activities- for cancer detection.

All kinds of treatments- surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy etc.

Hospital Based Cancer Registry

It has formulated National Guidelines for high- quality, cost- effective cancer treatment plan for 4 common cancers of our country.

Different training programs- for health professionals –histopathologists, gynecologists, nurses, technologists, community leaders etc.

Organizing tobacco prevention & cessation programmes in the community.

Participation in national activities- such as observance of World Cancer Day, World Pediatric Cancer Day, No Tobacco Day, Breast & Cervical cancer awareness month and chalking out of special Breast Cancer Screening Campaign.

Telemedicine services.

Publication of journal.

Palliative care services.

Academic activities.
